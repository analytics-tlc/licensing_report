Monthly Brief (May 2022)

What is this? This report gives an overview of how the New York City Taxi and Limousine Commission industries and operations have changed over the previous month.

In this section we compare data from April 2022 to March 2022.This report gives a breakdown into the different license types (i.e., Vehicle, Driver, and Businesses) and displays the current Active or In Storage numbers. The data displayed was last pulled on the first of this month, May 2022 and is used to show if certain license numbers have increased or decreased.Throughout this report, certain TLC-specific terms are used, which are defined below and at the start of each relevant section.

Definitions:

  • Active:An active license represents a license that is in good standing and currently able to be used by a TLC-licensed vehicle to conduct trips, or a license that has been suspended.
  • Good Standing: A license that is in good standing signifies that this license has no outstanding fees, penalties or fines that would lead to the suspension of that license.
  • Storage: A license that is in storage signifies that this license has been put “on-hold”. The license storage period cannot exceed one hundred eighty (180) days and implies the surrender of the TLC vehicle license plate associated with that license.

What is this? - This section provides a detailed look at how the main components of TLC industries have changed since the previous month. We look at the total number of active licenses across all license types, changes in active licenses for our FHV and Medallion vehicles, as well as changes within our network of drivers and bases.

Active Licenses: Last month there were 264,974 active licenses in the TLC between Vehicle, Medallion, Driver, and Business license types.

There were:

  • 94,957 Active FHVs(including: FHVs attached to HVFHS bases, livery bases, black car bases, and luxury bases), a 0.16% Decrease ▼ since the previous month's count of 95106 Active FHVs.
  • 7,410 Active Medallions, a 0.42% Decrease ▼ since the previous month count of 7441 Active Medallions

  • 161,776 Active Drivers, a 0.11% Increase ▲ since the previous month's count of 161601 Active Drivers

  • 831 Active Bases, a 1.66% Decrease ▼ since the previous month's count of 911 Active Businesses
